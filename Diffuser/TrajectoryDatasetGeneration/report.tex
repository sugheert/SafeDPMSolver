\subsection{Degree-4 Hyper-Ellipsoid Constraints}
Standard primitives were superseded with specifically engineered \textit{Degree-4 Super-ellipsoids (Hyper-Ellipsoids)}. In order to properly condition the continuous spaces enabling strict collision boundary gradients for the secondary optimizer, we utilized $L_4$ tensor-fields. The Approximate Signed Distance Function (SDF) of the surface scales mathematically:
\begin{equation}
D(p) \approx \min(r) \left( \left( \sum_{i} \left| \frac{p_i - c_i}{r_i} \right|^4 \right)^{\frac{1}{4}} - 1 \right)
\end{equation}
This establishes a functionally exact zero-level set formulation around the superellipse while remaining fully, smoothly differentiable anywhere across the coordinate structure, directly mapping topological clearances back into the GPMP cost derivatives.
